About Us

Our Story

We’re a team of students who believe learning history should feel like an adventure, not a chore. That’s why we created Navigate — a student-built online learning hub that helps peers explore the past in creative, interactive ways. Our site combines accessibility, collaboration, and design to bring history to life. From planning the color palette to coding every feature, we worked together to make learning engaging and inclusive for all.

Our Mission

At Navigate, our mission is to make history learning accessible, interactive, and collaborative. We believe students learn best when they’re curious, connected, and in control of their learning journey. By combining peer-to-peer learning, engaging digital resources, and real-time study sessions, Navigate turns history from memorization into exploration. Just like a compass guides travelers, Navigate helps students find direction in the stories that shaped our world.
