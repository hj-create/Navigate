flag Our Mission

Navigate through history with our personalized tutoring experience! Our tutors provide one-on-one help as well as feedback to help you get the grade you deserve. Offering multiple services, Navigate includes an interactive calendar for learners to book their sessions and add their next test dates, along with a student dashboard to track learning progress and a resources page with lessons, videos, quizzes and more. So what are you waiting for? Book a session with us now on the Scheduling page! Thank you.

stars Why Choose Us?

Our business is created by peers who underwent the same issues you are now. We have an AI chatbox that serves multiple purposes: explaining questions when the tutor is absent, providing key focus points for the student, and giving actionable feedback for both tutor and student. Students and tutors also have a normal chatbox to communicate about homework, schedule, and questions. Additionally, a voice reader feature ensures better accessibility and support during technical difficulties in sessions.
